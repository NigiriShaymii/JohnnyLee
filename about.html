Artist Statement - Johnny Lee

As a digital artist for over eight years, I showcase my own narratives and creations that share the social issues within the imaginative, fictional setting. Time after time, humanity clashes against itself which highlights the innocence and the danger of influence. Like fission and fusion, we continue to build and chip ourselves to become the person we are today. Here, I can share my work of escapism, reality, and resolvement, encouraging evolution of our consciousness and personality.
